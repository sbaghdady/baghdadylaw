SAMI S. BAGHDADY, ESQ.

Sami S. Baghdady founded Baghdady Law Offices in 1993 as a boutique law firm that focuses on real estate and business law. He was an elected Selectman of the Town of Belmont, Massachusetts, and former Chair of the Belmont Planning Board. Attorney Baghdady is the Past-President of The Real Estate Bar Association for Massachusetts (REBA). He is also the Chair of the Greater Boston Advisory Board of The Salvation Army. Attorney Baghdady has a B.A. in economics, magna cum laude, from Boston University and a J.D. from Cornell Law School. He is an attorney with over 30 years of experience at helping clients solve their problems and accomplish their objectives.
